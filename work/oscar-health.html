Oscar Health

During the summer of 2019, I joined Oscar Health, a midstage tech startup, as a Software Engineer Intern. As a member of their Digital Member Experience team, I had full ownership of the O4B Competitive Step Tracking project. To give some context, Oscar provides health insurance to individuals and small businesses, and their small business plan is called Oscar for Business (or O4B for short). Oscar also provides a step tracking feature for all members, allowing them to track their daily step count and earn $1 for each day their step goal is met. My project involved designing a leaderboard to encourage a friendly competition between coworkers on the O4B plan. The business goal of the project was to improve O4B member engagement. The project was built using React Native, JavaScript, Python, Flask, and SQLAlchemy.
